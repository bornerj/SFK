Use this file as a template to record architectural, technical, or product decisions.

Naming pattern:
- `DECISION-001.md`
- `DECISION-002.md`

When to use:
- stack choice;
- structural flow change;
- a decision that creates an important trade-off;
- replacement or discontinuation of a previous approach.

Status: ACTIVE | DEPRECATED
Date: YYYY-MM-DD
Context: Describe the problem, constraint, or opportunity that motivated the decision.
Decision: Record objectively what was decided.
Consequences: Explain expected impacts, gains, losses, risks, and side effects.
